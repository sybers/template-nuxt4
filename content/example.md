# Markdown demo page

Welcome to this demo page. It showcases **many Markdown features** you can use in your Nuxt Content project.

---

## Headings & text formatting

You can use different heading levels:

### Level 3 heading

Normal text with **bold emphasis** and *italic*.

You can also use ~~strikethrough~~ when needed.

---

## Lists

### Bullet list

- Simple item
- Item with **important text**
- Item with a nested list:
  - Sub item 1
  - Sub item 2

### Ordered list

1. First step
2. Second step
3. Third step
